Why it helps

Built for the people who pull these reports

Every route, one click

Pick one report or all six, run all 250+ routes or just a subset like 5, 99, 101.

One sign-in for all

A single SSO + MFA login covers every report and environment. On managed PCs it can even sign itself in.

Resumable & resilient

Interrupted runs pick up where they left off, with per-route timeouts, automatic retries, and full logs.

Consolidate & compare

Merge per-route exports into one workbook, and build discrepancy reports comparing TSMIS and TSN data.

Optional fast mode

Run several browsers in parallel for a 2.5–3×+ speedup when you need a full set in a hurry.

Portable & self-contained

Runs from any writable folder and leaves nothing behind. Updates itself in place when a new version ships.

How it works

Three steps, then walk away

Download & unzip

Grab the latest release, unzip anywhere writable, and double-click TSMIS Exporter.exe.

Sign in once

Log in with your Caltrans SSO + MFA. Your session is saved and reused until it expires.

Pick & export

Tick the reports and routes, hit Start, and the tool exports every route for you.
