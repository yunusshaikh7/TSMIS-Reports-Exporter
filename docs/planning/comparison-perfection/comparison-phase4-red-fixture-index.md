## Non-negotiable method

- Perfect comparison behavior is the oracle, not compatibility with an old workbook,
  frozen test, historical count, or prior implementation.
- Every finding needs its own independently red reproduction, green correction gate,
  owning production modules, dependent invariants, and assigned real-data canary.
- A malformed, ambiguous, stale, wrong-role, partial, or cancelled input cannot certify
  `match`, green, or definitive differences.
- Any changed real count is a stop condition until exact member identity, producer/code
  versions, and a cell-level delta explain it.
- One parser/report family lands per semantic batch. Shared helpers do not replace each
  family's focused gates and real canary.

1. stable finding ID and one-sentence false claim;

5. proposed focused red/green check filename;
6. positive control and adversarial mutations;
7. real canary binding and exact hard stop; and
